- **Shipped a new, mechanically-distinct game: Poise** (`games/poise/`). Verb =
  **balance** — the fresh verb the roadmap called for (all prior verbs: steer,
  time-a-catch, thrust, flip-match, aim-and-bounce, precision-stack, keep-aloft). A
  ball-on-a-tilting-beam: you tilt the beam (← → / A D, or slide on touch), the ball
  rolls under the gravity component, keep it from rolling off either end and roll it
