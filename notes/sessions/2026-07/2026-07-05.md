### Ship — new game **Arc** (charge-and-release power lob)
- Verb: **judge power** — distinct from every existing game (steer, time-a-catch, thrust,
  flip-match, aim-bounce, stack, keep-aloft, balance, mirror). A launcher fires at a fixed
  45°; hold to build power, release to lob; land on the pad. No aim, no bounce.
- **Depth = precision combo:** centre **bullseye** doubles points; consecutive lands grow a
  ×1…×6 multiplier; a miss breaks the streak *and* costs one of three lives → a genuine
  safe-vs-greedy read each throw. Pads shrink + spread widens by stage.
- Built to the full quality bar (copied ink-bloom's shape): pure `arc.core.js` (the 45°
  range formula `landingX = v²/G` decides the outcome; the shell arc is cosmetic and lands
  exactly there), external-module `arc.shell.js` with a classic-script boot fallback,
  guarded render loop + global error logging, `arc.core.test.js` (**26 tests**: helpers +
  power↔distance round-trip, reset invariants, deterministic in-bounds pad spawning, stages
  + multiplier, the `lob` loop incl. frame-exact death, determinism under seed, a self-play
  winnability run, full meta layer + near-miss), README, package.json, and a generated
