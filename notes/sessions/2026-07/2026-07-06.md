Echo Chamber) and brought it onto the pattern. Ink Bloom shows one mote at a time, so a
"formation" here is a short **sequence of mote *spawn* patterns** (where the next motes appear),
copied in shape from Polarity's reference build but flavoured botanically:

- **`FORMATIONS` pool** (core): Scatter + Drift (calm, roomy, stage 0), **Vine** (motes step
  along a line — you sweep across the field, stage 0, notable), **Ring** (a wide loop, stage 1,
  notable), **Thicket** (a tight cluster you thread the growing trail through, stage 1, notable),
  **Spectrum** (a rare mostly-prism greed-rush — the late crescendo, stage 2, notable).
- **Progression drives variation:** `pickFormation(cfg, stage, rng, prevId)` is a stage-weighted,
  seeded pick over the `minStage`-eligible pool (soft-avoids immediate repeats); climbing stages
  opens the pool and weights toward the demanding patterns late.
- **`loadFormation`** fills a per-formation `moteQueue` (specs are normalised `{nx,ny,prism}`);
  **`spawnMote`** refactored to pull one spec at a time (loading a fresh formation when spent),
  mapping to the padded field and clamping. `tick` now returns a `formation` name when a notable
  formation's head mote becomes the active target; the shell flashes a quiet `#formCue` (calm
  patterns pass silently, honours reduced-motion). Start-panel copy names the pattern set.
- **Tests:** +10 pure-core (pool well-formed, stage-gated deterministic `pickFormation`,
  distinct-seeds→distinct-structure + same-seed determinism, mote bounds, queue-never-empties,
